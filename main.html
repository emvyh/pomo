Our Logo

The word pomodoro means a sauce that is created from tomatoes. This is why our logo is tomatoes!

Pomodoro Technique

Pomodoro is a technique that involves timers that consist of 25 minutes intervals followed by a 5 minute break. Repeat this process 5 more times and end it with a 15 minute long break. This is a technique that helps users focus, time management, productivity and more. This process was developed by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980s.

How to use our timers

  • Start a task and conintue to do the task for 25 minutes
  • After it ends, take a 5 minute break
  • Repeat this process 5 more times but instead of a 5 minute break, take a 15 minute break
  • Note! Our timers only support up to 60 minutes.
